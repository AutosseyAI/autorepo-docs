---
title: 'Limitations'
---

- Autorepo indexes your repo before running tasks to provide the best possible responses. This will take longer the larger your repo is.
- Autorepo may not fully understand directories with more than 100 items.
- Autorepo may not fully understand files with more than 100,000 [tokens](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/4936856-what-are-tokens-and-how-to-count-them).
